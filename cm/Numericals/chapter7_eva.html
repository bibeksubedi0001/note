Earned Value Analysis (EVA)

4 Solved Problems 2070–2078

EVA Technique Summary

Key EVA Metrics & Formulas

Three Fundamental Values
MetricFull NameMeaning
BCWS (PV)Budgeted Cost of Work Scheduled (Planned Value)Budget for work planned to be done
BCWP (EV)Budgeted Cost of Work Performed (Earned Value)Budget for work actually done
ACWP (AC)Actual Cost of Work Performed (Actual Cost)Money actually spent
Variance Analysis

$$\text{CV (Cost Variance)} = \text{EV} - \text{AC}$$ Positive = Under budget

$$\text{SV (Schedule Variance)} = \text{EV} - \text{PV}$$ Positive = Ahead of schedule

Performance Indices

$$\text{CPI} = \frac{\text{EV}}{\text{AC}}$$ >1 = Under budget, <1 = Over budget

$$\text{SPI} = \frac{\text{EV}}{\text{PV}}$$ >1 = Ahead, <1 = Behind schedule

Forecasting

$$\text{EAC} = \frac{\text{BAC}}{\text{CPI}}$$ Estimate at Completion

$$\text{ETC} = \text{EAC} - \text{AC}$$ Estimate to Complete

$$\text{TCPI} = \frac{\text{BAC} - \text{EV}}{\text{BAC} - \text{AC}}$$ To-Complete Performance Index

BAC = Budget at Completion (total project budget)

Q1. EVA with Bar Chart 2078 Kartik

Perform EVA and comment on the performance for the given project. The progress was monitored and recorded at the end of 5th day. [7 marks]

Budget & Schedule:

B10,0005Day 1–5

E6,0005Day 5–9

Status at end of Day 5:

Activity% CompleteActual Expenditure (Rs.)

D0%0
E30%3,500
F0%0

Solution

Step 1: Calculate BCWS (Planned Value) at Day 5

What % of each activity was scheduled to be done by Day 5?

ActivityBudgetScheduled % by Day 5BCWS
A5,000100% (Day 1–3, all done)5,000
B10,000100% (Day 1–5, all done)10,000
C15,00060% (3 of 5 days: Day 3,4,5)9,000
D7,00040% (2 of 5 days: Day 4,5)2,800
E6,00020% (1 of 5 days: Day 5)1,200
F2,0000% (starts Day 8)0
Total BCWS (PV)28,000
Step 2: Calculate BCWP (Earned Value) at Day 5

Budget × actual % completed:

ActivityBudget% CompleteBCWP
A5,000100%5,000
B10,00080%8,000
C15,00060%9,000
D7,0000%0
E6,00030%1,800
F2,0000%0
Total BCWP (EV)23,800
Step 3: Calculate ACWP (Actual Cost)

$$\text{ACWP} = 5{,}000 + 9{,}000 + 8{,}000 + 0 + 3{,}500 + 0 = \textbf{25,500}$$

Step 4: Variance Analysis

$$\text{CV} = \text{EV} - \text{AC} = 23{,}800 - 25{,}500 = \textcolor{red}{-1{,}700}$$ → Over budget!

$$\text{SV} = \text{EV} - \text{PV} = 23{,}800 - 28{,}000 = \textcolor{red}{-4{,}200}$$ → Behind schedule!

Step 5: Performance Indices

$$\text{CPI} = \frac{\text{EV}}{\text{AC}} = \frac{23{,}800}{25{,}500} = \textcolor{red}{0.933}$$ → <1, Over budget

$$\text{SPI} = \frac{\text{EV}}{\text{PV}} = \frac{23{,}800}{28{,}000} = \textcolor{red}{0.850}$$ → <1, Behind schedule

Step 6: Forecasting

BAC = Total Budget = 5,000+10,000+15,000+7,000+6,000+2,000 = Rs.45,000

$$\text{EAC} = \frac{\text{BAC}}{\text{CPI}} = \frac{45{,}000}{0.933} = \textbf{Rs.48,231}$$

$$\text{ETC} = \text{EAC} - \text{AC} = 48{,}231 - 25{,}500 = \textbf{Rs.22,731}$$

★ The project is OVER BUDGET by Rs.1,700 (CV = −1,700)
★ The project is BEHIND SCHEDULE by Rs.4,200 worth of work (SV = −4,200)
★ CPI = 0.933, SPI = 0.850
★ Estimated Cost at Completion = Rs.48,231 (vs budget of Rs.45,000)

Q2. Road Construction EVA 2077 Chaitra 2074 Magh

Sinoma company of China has planned to prepare a Subgrade for Koteshwor to Kalanki road section of 8.7 km length of ring road to be accomplished within 1.5 years at a cost of 1.5 million per kilometer. The data were taken after 6 months of commencement of the work, the work found to be 2.3 km completed by expending 4.6 million rupees. Perform EVA and draw your conclusion regarding the Chinese performance in Nepalese domain by calculating each component of EVA. [6 marks]

Solution

Given

Total Length = 8.7 km, Total Duration = 1.5 years = 18 months

Rate = Rs.1.5 million/km

BAC = 8.7 × 1.5 = Rs.13.05 million

At 6 months: Work done = 2.3 km, Actual cost = Rs.4.6 million

Step 1: Calculate PV (BCWS)

Planned work in 6 months = (6/18) × 8.7 = 2.9 km

$$\text{PV} = 2.9 \times 1.5 = \textbf{Rs.4.35 million}$$

Step 2: Calculate EV (BCWP)

$$\text{EV} = 2.3 \times 1.5 = \textbf{Rs.3.45 million}$$

Step 3: AC (ACWP)

$\text{AC} = \text{Rs.}4.6 \text{ million}$ (given)

Step 4: Variance Analysis

$$\text{CV} = \text{EV} - \text{AC} = 3.45 - 4.6 = \textcolor{red}{-1.15 \text{ million}}$$ → Over budget

$$\text{SV} = \text{EV} - \text{PV} = 3.45 - 4.35 = \textcolor{red}{-0.90 \text{ million}}$$ → Behind schedule

Step 5: Performance Indices

$$\text{CPI} = \frac{\text{EV}}{\text{AC}} = \frac{3.45}{4.6} = \textcolor{red}{0.75}$$

$$\text{SPI} = \frac{\text{EV}}{\text{PV}} = \frac{3.45}{4.35} = \textcolor{red}{0.793}$$

Step 6: Forecasting

$$\text{EAC} = \frac{\text{BAC}}{\text{CPI}} = \frac{13.05}{0.75} = \textbf{Rs.17.4 million}$$

$$\text{ETC} = \text{EAC} - \text{AC} = 17.4 - 4.6 = \textbf{Rs.12.8 million}$$

$$\text{Estimated time} = \frac{\text{Duration}}{\text{SPI}} = \frac{18}{0.793} = \textbf{22.7 months}$$

★ Project is Over Budget by Rs.1.15M and Behind Schedule by 0.6 km
★ CPI = 0.75, SPI = 0.793
★ Estimated total cost = Rs.17.4 million (original: Rs.13.05M — 33% overrun)
★ Estimated completion = 22.7 months (original: 18 months — 4.7 months delay)
